## Google Search Findings
### Overview of OpenCals
**OpenCals** is a modern online booking platform designed primarily for service-based businesses. It employs AI-powered setup and offers features that streamline scheduling and staff management while allowing for seamless integrations with other tools.

### Key Features
1. **AI-Powered Setup**: OpenCals allows businesses to set up their services, schedules, and availability in under 10 minutes using an AI assistant.
2. **Multi-Location & Staff Management**: Users can manage multiple locations and staff schedules efficiently.
3. **Flexible Scheduling**: The platform supports various booking types, accommodates group bookings, and settings for flexible service durations.
4. **Integration Capabilities**: OpenCals integrates with popular services like Google Calendar, Zoom, and Google Meet.
5. **Customizable Interface**: Businesses can customize booking widgets, emails, and notifications.
6. **Payment Processing**: Integrated payment processing through Stripe or Shopify Payments.
7. **User-Friendly Customer Portal**: Customers manage their appointments through a self-service portal.
8. **Analytics & Reporting**: Businesses can access insights regarding bookings and service performance.

### Use Cases
- **Beauty Services**: For salons and spas managing multiple appointments.
- **Fitness and Health**: For gyms and personal trainers.
- **Education**: For tutoring services and language schools.
- **Professional Services**: For consultants and freelancers.
- **Hospitality**: For booking-related services like car rentals.

### Pricing Structure
- **Elastic Plan**: Starting at **$0.99 per appointment**.
- **Custom Plan**: Priced at **$15 per month**.

### Notable Information
- **Launch Date**: July 3, 2024.
- **International Availability**: Supports multiple languages and currencies.
- **Free Trial and Support**: Offers a free trial period and dedicated support.
