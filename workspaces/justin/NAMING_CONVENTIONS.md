# Naming Conventions

## Date-First Files
Use ISO date prefixes for time-bound outputs:
- `YYYY-MM-DD-daily-brief.md`
- `YYYY-MM-DD-topic-research.md`
- `YYYY-MM-DD-open-loops.md`

## Business-Specific Files
- `battle-brew-pasteurization-sop-v1.md`
- `battles-budz-investor-update-draft-v1.md`
- `homegrower-circle-webinar-script-v1.md`

## Versioning
- Use `-v1`, `-v2`, etc. for iterative drafts.
- Use `-draft-` in filename until approved/finalized.

## Status Suffixes (Optional)
- `-draft`
- `-review`
- `-final`

## Formatting Rules
- Lowercase
- Hyphen-separated words
- No spaces
- Keep names specific but concise
