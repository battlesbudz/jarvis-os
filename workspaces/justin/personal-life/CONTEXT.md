# Personal Life Context

## Purpose
Organize personal and family-related planning across household, routines, health, and finances.

## When to Use
Use for non-business planning, personal commitments, and life-admin organization.

## Read First
- Most recent files in relevant subfolder (`family/`, `health/`, `finances/`, `routines/`, `home/`)
- Current planning docs in `daily-command-center/` when coordination is needed

## Output Types
Personal plans, trackers, checklists, and decision notes.

## Do Not Load By Default
Do not load business folders unless a task explicitly spans life + business scheduling.
