# Workspace Map

## Folder Purpose and Routing Hints

- `daily-command-center/`
  - Purpose: daily execution control (priorities, open loops, tasks, briefs, decisions).
  - Route from: ORACLE, SCOUT, PRIME synthesis.
  - Outputs: daily briefs, task snapshots, decision logs.

- `personal-life/`
  - Purpose: non-business planning domains.
  - Route from: ORACLE (planning) + ECHO (memory continuity).
  - Outputs: routines, household plans, personal admin trackers.

- `business/`
  - Purpose: operating files per business line.
  - Route from: HERALD (comms), FORGE (docs), ATLAS (research), ORACLE (planning).
  - Outputs: SOP drafts, investor updates, compliance notes, partner briefs.

- `content-studio/`
  - Purpose: content ideation to final assets.
  - Route from: FORGE + HERALD.
  - Outputs: scripts, drafts, final posts, email variants, repurposed assets.

- `research/`
  - Purpose: structured research by topic.
  - Route from: ATLAS.
  - Outputs: dated briefs, citations, competitor/legal/grants notes.

- `production/`
  - Purpose: execution artifacts for builds and deliverables.
  - Route from: FORGE + ORACLE + PRIME synthesis.
  - Outputs: briefs, specs, builds, packaged outputs.

- `templates/`
  - Purpose: reusable drafting scaffolds.
  - Route from: FORGE/HERALD/ATLAS.
  - Outputs: new drafts instantiated from templates.

- `archive/`
  - Purpose: inactive or superseded workspace materials.
  - Route from: manual archival only.

## Naming and Destination Guidance
- Follow `NAMING_CONVENTIONS.md` for all new artifacts.
- Put outputs in the most specific subfolder possible.
- Do not place final deliverables in root workspace folder.
