# Personal Life Context

## Purpose
This is the personal operating room for Battles' non-business life: family, health, home, routines, finances, commitments, and general life admin. It should help Jarvis reduce friction and protect attention, not overcomplicate private life with business-style artifacts.

## Route Here When
- The user asks about family, home, health, finances, routines, chores, appointments, or personal planning.
- A business plan needs to account for real-life capacity.
- The user needs a low-friction checklist, tracker, or routine.
- Jarvis needs ECHO-style continuity for personal preferences or context.

## Read First
1. The relevant subfolder when present: `family/`, `health/`, `finances/`, `routines/`, or `home/`.
2. `daily-command-center/current-state.md` when personal context affects today's plan.
3. `daily-command-center/open-loops.md` for unresolved personal tasks.

## Prefer
- Simple checklists.
- Tiny next steps.
- Clear boundaries between personal and business work.
- Gentle ADHD-aware planning when the user seems overloaded.
- Draft-only mode for sensitive financial, health, or family decisions.

## Skip Unless Needed
- Business folders unless the task explicitly crosses life and work.
- Research folders unless a health, finance, or legal question needs evidence.
- Content or production folders unless creating a public artifact from a personal story.

## Pipeline
1. Identify the life domain.
2. Capture the desired outcome.
3. Break it into the smallest next action.
4. Place any follow-up in `daily-command-center/open-loops.md`.
5. Save durable preferences or recurring patterns only when appropriate.

## Output Types
- Personal plans
- Household checklists
- Routine trackers
- Appointment prep notes
- Finance admin checklists
- Family/life decision notes

## Approval Boundaries
Do not make purchases, schedule appointments, send messages, alter accounts, or treat health/financial notes as professional advice without explicit approval.

## Handoff Back To PRIME
Return the personal domain, next action, any open loop created, and whether the daily plan should change.
