## Product Launch Gate
Do not move a product to launch until these are confirmed:
- License/legal path.
- Production partner or facility process.
- Testing requirements.
- Packaging and label requirements.
- Inventory/recordkeeping workflow.
- Cost and margin estimate.
- Retail/distribution target.
- Approval from Battles.

## Next Best Actions
1. Confirm the legal/compliance path for each product.
2. Identify what can be produced internally versus through partners.
3. Estimate cash required for first sellable batch.
4. Identify retail/distribution targets.
5. Pick first product by fastest compliant revenue path, not brand preference.
