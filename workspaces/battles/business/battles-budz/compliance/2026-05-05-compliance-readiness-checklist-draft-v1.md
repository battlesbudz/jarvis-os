# Compliance Readiness Checklist

## Status
- Compliance maturity: Needs confirmation.
- Required SOP set: Needs confirmation.

## Purpose
This draft checklist organizes compliance readiness work. It is not legal advice, not an official compliance manual, and not approved for operational use until Battles explicitly approves it and qualified reviewers verify it where needed.

## Approval Rule
Jarvis must ask for explicit approval before treating any SOP, compliance plan, regulator response, license document, operating process, or official recordkeeping method as final.
