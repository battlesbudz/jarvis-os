# Facility Readiness Checklist

## Status
- Facility selected: Needs confirmation.
- Lease/ownership status: Needs confirmation.
- Inspection readiness: Needs confirmation.
- Production readiness: Needs confirmation.
- Target outcome: Facility blockers are visible, sequenced, and tied to licensing/compliance/product launch.

## Purpose
This draft checklist helps Jarvis organize facility readiness for Battles Budz. It should be verified against the actual license requirements, local rules, lease/buildout constraints, insurance needs, and any OCM or inspection guidance.

## Approval Rule
Jarvis must ask for explicit approval before contacting landlords, contractors, inspectors, regulators, vendors, insurers, partners, or making any facility commitment.

## Production Capability Questions
1. What products can legally and practically be produced in this facility?
2. What must be outsourced to processors, co-packers, cultivators, or other partners?
3. What equipment is required for first revenue only?
4. What equipment can wait until revenue begins?
5. What facility items are required before final approval or inspection?
