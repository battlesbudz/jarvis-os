# Licensing Readiness Checklist

## Status
- Stage: Needs confirmation from Battles.
- OCM/final approval status: Needs confirmation.
- Blocking issue: Needs confirmation.
- Target outcome: Final approval path is clear, tracked, and ready for action.

## Purpose
This draft checklist helps Jarvis organize licensing work for Battles Budz. It is not legal advice and should not be treated as an official licensing submission, regulatory position, or compliance certification.

## Approval Rule
Jarvis must ask Battles for explicit approval before sending anything to OCM, regulators, attorneys, consultants, partners, investors, or external reviewers.

## Read Before Acting
- Current OCM portal/application status.
- Any deficiency notices, conditional approval letters, inspection notes, or regulator messages.
- Facility readiness notes.
- Compliance readiness notes.
- Attorney/consultant guidance, if available.

| Professional review | Unknown | Attorney/consultant feedback | Battles | Decide if outside review is needed before submission |

## Questions Jarvis Should Ask First
1. What is the exact current OCM/licensing stage?
2. Has OCM given a deficiency, checklist, inspection requirement, or final blocker list?
3. Is the facility already selected, leased/owned, and inspection-ready?
4. Are there any deadlines, renewal dates, inspection dates, or response windows?
5. Who has authority to submit or respond officially?
