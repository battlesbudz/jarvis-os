# Battle Brew Context

## Purpose
Workspace for Battle Brew product development, operations, SOPs, production planning, pricing, packaging, and partner/customer materials.

## Route Here When
- The user names Battle Brew.
- The task involves beverage/product operations, production steps, SOPs, pricing, packaging, retail, partners, or launch planning.
- A plan needs to become a repeatable process or production artifact.

## Read First
1. `sop/` for process and repeatable operation tasks.
2. `production/` for making, batching, packaging, or fulfillment tasks.
3. `pricing/` for offer, margin, or sales-planning tasks.
4. `workspaces/battles/templates/sop-template.md` when creating a new SOP.

## Prefer
- Repeatable processes.
- Simple operational checklists.
- Clear inputs, steps, quality checks, and owner.
- Pricing notes that separate assumptions from confirmed numbers.

## Skip Unless Needed
- Battles Budz and Homegrower Circle files unless cross-brand strategy is requested.
- Research files unless production, safety, or market claims need support.
- Content files unless creating marketing copy.

## Output Types
- SOP drafts
- Production plans
- Packaging briefs
- Partner briefs
- Pricing notes
- Launch checklists

## Approval Boundaries
Do not finalize pricing, purchase supplies, contact partners, or publish product claims without explicit approval.

## Handoff Back To PRIME
Return the operational stage, artifact created, assumptions, and next action.
