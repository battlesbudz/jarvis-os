# Homegrower Circle Context

## Purpose
Workspace for Homegrower Circle education, community, courses, webinars, interviews, sponsors, and member engagement.

## Route Here When
- The user names Homegrower Circle.
- The task involves education, courses, webinars, community programming, interviews, sponsors, or grower-facing content.
- Research or business strategy needs to become a curriculum, event, or community asset.

## Read First
1. `courses/` and `webinars/` for curriculum and event tasks.
2. `community/` for engagement, member experience, or recurring programming.
3. Sponsor/interview files when the task involves external partners.
4. `workspaces/battles/content-studio/` only when converting the work into publishable content.

## Prefer
- Practical educational outcomes.
- Clear audience level: beginner, intermediate, advanced, sponsor, or community member.
- Simple curriculum structures.
- Community trust and compliance-aware language.

## Skip Unless Needed
- Other business lines unless cross-brand strategy is requested.
- Personal-life files.
- Production files unless the course/webinar is moving into build/output stage.

## Output Types
- Course outlines
- Webinar scripts
- Sponsor briefs
- Interview briefs
- Community content plans
- Member resource drafts

## Approval Boundaries
Do not contact sponsors, publish curriculum, or make regulated cultivation/compliance claims without explicit approval and source support.

## Handoff Back To PRIME
Return the program type, audience, artifact created, and next action.
