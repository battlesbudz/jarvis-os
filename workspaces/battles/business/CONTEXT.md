# Business Context

## Purpose
Top-level operating area for Battles business entities and related strategic/operational outputs.

## When to Use
Use for company operations, compliance, investors, product decisions, partnerships, and business communications.

## Read First
- Relevant line-of-business context (`battles-budz/`, `battle-brew/`, `homegrower-circle/`)
- Most recent project/status docs in the selected business line

## Output Types
Operational plans, investor updates, SOP drafts, partner briefs, and compliance notes.

## Do Not Load By Default
Avoid loading unrelated business lines unless cross-company strategy is requested.
